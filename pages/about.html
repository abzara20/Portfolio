About Me

Pike Robodevils
Pike Summer Stretch

I was born in Chicago, Illinois, but have lived in Indianapolis a majority of my life. I graduated from IUPUI in 2022 with a Bachelors in Informatics and another in Media Arts and Design, with a focus on Full Stack Web Development. For a long time, my focus has been in Graphic Design, where I have certifications in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Indesign. With this I pursued a minor in Studio Arts and Technology, so I have a strong background in artistry and design.

Some of my activites involve teaching students about STEM and design, such as being a mentor in the iDEW summer workshops program. I had also mentored a high school robotics team for a time called the Pike Robodevils in Marketing and Promotions, teaching students how to use the Adobe Creative Suite.

My experiences have led me to build strong teamwork and communication skills, in addition to problem solving skills and an eye for detail. I am mostly interested in pushing further and gaining more experience in field of Web design and development.
